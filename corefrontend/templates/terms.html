5. Trade-In Program – Device Grade Definitions

During trade-in inspection, your device will be assessed and graded based on its physical and functional condition.

Like New

  • Condition: No visible signs of wear or damage.
  • Functionality: 100% functional.
  • Other: Must include the original box and accessories.
  • Battery (refurbished phones): Minimum cycle count of 500.

Good

  • Condition: Minor visible wear (light scratches or scuffs).
  • Functionality: Fully functional but may require minor repairs (e.g., screen protector replacement or small component repair).
  • Other: Original box preferred but not required.
  • Battery (refurbished phones): Minimum cycle count of 500.

Poor

  • Condition: Significant visible wear and/or damage (e.g., deep scratches, dents, cracks in non-critical areas).
  • Functionality: Powers on but has significant functional issues (e.g., faulty buttons, poor speaker quality, camera defects).
  • Other: Original box not required.
  • Battery (refurbished phones): Minimum cycle count of 500.

Faulty / Broken

  • Condition: Severe physical damage (e.g., shattered screen, bent frame, major casing damage).
  • Functionality: Does not power on, has major hardware failure (e.g., no display, water damage), or is activation locked (iCloud or Google FRP).
  • Other: Considered for parts or repair. Original box irrelevant.

Process

  • Obtain an online quote (valid for 7 days).

  • Once accepted via email, WhatsApp, or call, payment will be processed and will take 24 hours to reflect in your bank. The decision cannot be amended. You will lose rights to your device once you have accepted the offer.

Important Notes

  • You must remove all personal data (e.g., iCloud or Google Account) before sending your device.
  • If you reject the adjusted final offer, a £10 return fee applies.
  • The free shipping label provides maximum insurance coverage of £150.
  • All devices undergo IMEI validation and checks for liquid damage and activation lock status.
